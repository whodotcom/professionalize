Professionalize provides helpful tips to edit a body of text and Professionalizes any written communication through a Chrome Extension.

Why Use It?

Easy to Use Chrome Extension

With the click of a button, you can quickly professionalize your writing. The Extension is conveniently located at the top of the Chrome browser. Copy and paste your selected text in the Chrome Extension and you are on your way to more professional communication.

Helpful Tips For Your Writing

Just enter your text into the Chrome Extension and Professionalize will give you real time tips and important information about the way you write. Use this information to then better your emails, messages, or any written communication.

Increase Your Respectibility

With better writing, supervisors and professors will gain more respect for what you have to say. Whether it's a submission to your boss, a question to your professor, or a reply to a prospective client, Professionalize can Professionalize your writing!

What Does It Do?

Contraction Correction

Contractions are automatically corrected in the Professionalize Extension. Not an apostrophe in sight!

Grammatical Replacement

Professionalize will check for grammatical errors and replace them in your text with the click of a button!

directions_run

Passive Voice Prohibitor

If you are speaking in a passive tone, Professionalize will suggest alternative ways to correct this and bring things to the present tense.
