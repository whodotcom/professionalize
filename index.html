Better your writing with the click of a button.

Professionalize provides helpful tips to specifically edit a body of text for any professional communication through an easy-to-use Chrome Extension.

Easy to Use Chrome Extension

With the click of a button, you can quickly professionalize your writing. The Extension is conveniently located at the top of the Chrome browser. Copy and paste your selected text in the Chrome Extension and you are on your way to more professional communication.

Helpful Tips For Your Writing

Just enter your text into the Chrome Extension and Professionalize will give you real time tips and important information about the way you write. Use this information to then better your emails, messages, or any written communication.

Increase Your Respectibility

With better writing, supervisors and professors will gain more respect for what you have to say. Whether it's a submission to your boss, a question to your professor, or a reply to a prospective client, Professionalize will Professionalize your writing!
